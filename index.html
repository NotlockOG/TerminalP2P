TERMS OF SERVICE & PRIVACY POLICY

1. Service Description

P2P-CHAT is a peer-to-peer encrypted chat application. All communications are sent directly between users' devices with end-to-end encryption.

2. Privacy & Monitoring

WE DO NOT AND CANNOT MONITOR YOUR CHATS. This service uses peer-to-peer technology with end-to-end encryption. All messages are sent directly between users' browsers. We have no servers that store, process, or can view your communications.

  • Messages are encrypted on your device
  • Transmitted directly to recipient's device
  • Decrypted only on recipient's device
  • No message history is stored anywhere
  • Signaling servers (if used) only coordinate connections, never see message content

3. User Responsibility

WE CANNOT CONTROL WHAT USERS SEND OR RECEIVE. By using this service, you acknowledge:

  • You are solely responsible for content you send
  • We cannot moderate, filter, or control any communications
  • We cannot recover or delete messages after they're sent
  • You must comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction
  • You must be 13 years or older (or age required in your country)

4. Prohibited Uses

You agree NOT to use this service for:

  • Illegal activities or content
  • Harassment, threats, or abuse
  • Sharing malware or malicious files
  • Impersonating others
  • Spamming or commercial solicitation
  • Child exploitation material (strictly prohibited and illegal)

5. Data Collection

We collect MINIMAL data:

  • Your chosen username (stored locally in your browser)
  • Connection metadata if using signaling server (IP addresses, connection times)
  • NO message content
  • NO files shared
  • NO chat history

6. Security

While we implement strong encryption (AES-256-GCM), no system is 100% secure. You use this service at your own risk. We are not responsible for:

  • Compromised devices
  • User error in sharing codes/passwords
  • Network security issues
  • Third-party interception

7. File Sharing

File sharing is peer-to-peer and direct. We do not scan, filter, or control files. Users are responsible for:

  • Scanning files for malware before opening
  • Ensuring they have rights to share files
  • Complying with copyright laws
  • Not sharing illegal content
